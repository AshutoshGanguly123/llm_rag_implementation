Step 1: Preparing ingredients
- Peel and clean a dozen shrimp, cut them in half and put in a gratin dish.
- Add about a cup of leftover bread (cubed), chopped garlic, scallions, chopped mushrooms, parsley, butter, olive oil, salt, and pepper.

Step 2: Mixing ingredients
- Mix the ingredients in the gratin dish thoroughly, ensuring an even spread of flavors.

Step 3: Baking
- Preheat the oven to 400 degrees Fahrenheit.
- Place the gratin dish in the oven and bake for about 18-20 minutes.

Step 4: Serving
- Once baked, remove the gratin dish from the oven.
- Serve the delicious gratin of shrimps with all the flavorful ingredients.
- Enjoy your meal!
