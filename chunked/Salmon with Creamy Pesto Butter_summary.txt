1. Preparation of Fish:
- Slice the fish fillet to make it the same thickness.
- Season with a sprinkle of salt on both sides.
- Drizzle a dash of oil on top of the fish.
- Bake the fish at 350Â°F for about 5 minutes.

2. Preparation of Pesto Sauce:
- Melt 1 tablespoon of butter in the microwave.
- Add 2 tablespoons of pesto to the melted butter.
- Optionally add a dash of pepper.

3. Serving:
- Serve the baked salmon with the prepared pesto sauce.
- Garnish with fresh basil and oregano.
- Enjoy the dish as an appetizer or first course.
