Step 1: Preparation of the sauce
- In a bowl, combine 3 tablespoons of mayonnaise, 1/4 cup sour cream, 1-2 teaspoons of horseradish, finely minced mild onion (about 1/4 cup), 1 tablespoon of chopped chives, and a dash of hot sauce.

Step 2: Seasoning and assembling the dish
- Add salt and pepper to taste.
- Gently fold in 8 ounces of lump crab meat.
- Chill the crab meat mixture in the fridge.
- Arrange a bed of lettuce in a bowl for serving.

Step 3: Plating
- Place a portion of the crab meat mixture on the lettuce bed.
- Garnish with a sprinkle of chopped chives.
- Serve the elegant crab meat dish as a first course.
