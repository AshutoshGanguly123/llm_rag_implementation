**Step 1: Preparation for Onion Sandwich**
Jacques Pepin shares his experience with James Beard and his onion sandwich recipe. He demonstrates cutting the bread rounds, spreading mayonnaise, placing onion slices, adding salt, assembling the sandwich, and garnishing with chives.

**Step 2: Preparation for Radish Sandwich**
Jacques Pepin introduces a radish sandwich inspired by the French radish, butter, and bread tradition. He shows slicing radishes thinly, filling the bread with slices, salting, assembling the sandwich with butter, trimming the edges, and garnishing with parsley.

**Step 3: Conclusion and Serving**
Jacques Pepin concludes by presenting the radish sandwich alongside the onion sandwich as delicious lunch options, encouraging viewers to enjoy the recipes.
